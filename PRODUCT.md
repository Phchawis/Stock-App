# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Medical lab technicians, stock supervisors, and lab administrators at the Thammasat University Hospital (TUH) Laboratory Center. They operate in a fast-paced clinical environment, needing quick, error-free stock lookups, receipts, issues, and report prints.

## Product Purpose
Track clinical reagents inventory, manage expiration alerts and low-stock triggers, enforce FEFO (First-Expired, First-Out) stock issuance, receive new chemical lots, and print professional monthly KPI reports.

## Brand Personality
Clinical, professional, reliable, precise, and secure. Reflects the rigorous and life-impacting nature of medical laboratory diagnostics.

## Anti-references
Generic warm-sand SaaS templates (cliché AI aesthetics), nested card layouts, side-stripe colored card borders, decorative glassmorphic overlays, and neon gradient text headings.

## Design Principles
1. **Clinical Precision & Contrast**: Prioritize high legibility under varying laboratory light levels. Text must have a strict WCAG AA contrast ratio.
2. **Actionable Expiry (FEFO first)**: Stock expiration dates are critical safety indicators. The queue of active lots must display chronological expiration order with bold, color-coded severity tags.
3. **High Density, Zero Clutter**: Medical professionals need to see full registration specs, current lots, and transaction logs side-by-side without endless scrolling or jumping between pages.
4. **Formal Printable Integrity**: Monthly reports must output as clean, clean-bordered, corporate A4 vector documents suitable for academic or management review.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Strict compliance with WCAG AA standards (contrast ≥4.5:1 for body and labels). Keyboard-accessible inputs, large click targets, and distinct status icons alongside colored badges (do not rely on color alone for critical alerts).
